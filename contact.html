Frequently Asked Questions

When is the Museum open?

The musuem is open from 9.00am to 5.00pm daily

Do I have to pay the full price for admission?

We offer discounts for students, seniors, and military members. See our website for details.

How much is admission to the Museum?

Tickets are $10 online, or slightly more at the door

Is there a time when admission reduced?

We offer free admission on the tuesday from 9am to 10am. However, it can get crowded during these times, so be sure to plan accordingly!

Can anyone bypass the Admissions line?

Museum members often have a dedicated entrance with shorter wait times.
