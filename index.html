History of our art gallery

Over 75 years of fostering artistic expression.

Immerse yourself in the rich history of our gallery, a vibrant hub for artistic exploration since 1948.
The gallery has been a cornerstone of the art community for over seven decades, showcasing a diverse range of artistic styles and movements. We have championed the works of emerging and established artists, providing a platform for them to share their creativity with the world.
